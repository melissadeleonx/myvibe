Welcome to uniVVV

Developed as a visionaryCS50 project by melissadeleonx, uniVVV is a futuristic social media platform that goes beyond the ordinary, transcending terrestrial boundaries to forge connections that resonate across the cosmos.

At its core, uniVVV aspires to craft an unparalleled and immersive space where individuals from diverse corners of the galaxy converge. Within this cosmic social hub, users curate their virtual homes on officially recognized planets, giving rise to an extraordinary and immersive interstellar odyssey.

Welcome to uniVVV, your journey unfolds in the vast expanse of the universe.
